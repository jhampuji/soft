About Us

What make us the best?

Soft Skills Consultants (Mauritius) Ltd was established in March 2010 with the primary objective of delivering soft skills training to the corporate sector. The company is registered with the Mauritius Qualifications Authority (MQA) and is also a licensed recruitment agency under the Ministry of Labour, Industrial Relations, Employment, and Training.
